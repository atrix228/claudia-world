# Privacy Policy — Claudia World

**Last Updated: June 28, 2026**

EverStart Creation ("we", "us", "our") operates the Claudia World mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use our App.

## Summary

**Claudia World does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers.** The App operates entirely on your device. We have no servers, no analytics, no tracking, and no user accounts.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- ❌ No personal information (name, email, phone number)
- ❌ No usage analytics or telemetry
- ❌ No crash reports sent to us
- ❌ No advertising identifiers
- ❌ No location tracking
- ❌ No cookies or web tracking
- ❌ No user accounts or sign-ups required

## How The App Works

Claudia World is an AI assistant client that connects **directly** from your device to third-party AI API providers (such as OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, or other OpenAI-compatible services). 

### API Keys
- Your API key is stored **locally on your device** in the app's private storage
- Your API key is sent **directly** to the AI provider you configure — never to our servers
- We do not have access to your API keys

### Device Permissions
The App requests the following Android permissions to enable AI tool functionality. **All permission usage is local — no data leaves your device except to your configured AI provider:**

| Permission | Purpose | Data Sent Externally? |
|---|---|---|
| **Phone (CALL_PHONE)** | Allows AI to place calls on your behalf | No — calls are made through your phone's dialer |
| **SMS (READ_SMS, SEND_SMS)** | Allows AI to read and send SMS messages | No — SMS data stays on device |
| **Contacts (READ_CONTACTS)** | Allows AI to search your contacts | No — contact data stays on device |
| **Calendar (READ/WRITE_CALENDAR)** | Allows AI to read and create calendar events | No — calendar data stays on device |
| **Location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION)** | Provides location context to AI | Location text may be sent to your AI provider as part of a conversation |
| **Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO)** | Voice input for AI conversations | Audio may be sent to your AI provider for transcription |
| **Storage (READ/WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE)** | Allows AI to read and manage files | No — file operations are local |
| **Notifications (BIND_NOTIFICATION_LISTENER_SERVICE)** | Allows AI to read your notifications | No — notification data stays on device |
| **Clipboard** | Allows AI to read/write clipboard | No — clipboard data stays on device |

**You can deny any permission** and the App will still function — the corresponding AI tool will simply be unavailable.

### Conversation Data
- All conversations are stored **locally on your device**
- Conversation content is sent to your configured AI provider for processing
- We do not have access to your conversations
- You can delete all conversation history from within the App

### Third-Party AI Providers
When you use the App, your messages are sent to the AI provider you configure (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic, Google). Each provider has their own privacy policy:
- [OpenAI Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy)
- [Anthropic Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy)
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

**We are not responsible for how these third-party providers handle your data.** We recommend reviewing their privacy policies before use.

## Data Security

- All data is stored in the App's private storage area, protected by Android's app sandboxing
- API keys are stored using Android's secure storage mechanisms
- No data is transmitted to our servers — ever
